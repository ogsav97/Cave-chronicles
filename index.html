Crafting

How to Play

  • Move: WASD / Left Joystick (mobile)
  • Look: Mouse drag / Right side drag (mobile)
  • Interact: E or tap prompt
  • Build Mode: Click Build → choose item in Craft then place ghost by tapping ground. Confirm with Place.
  • Gather: Approach trees/rocks and press E (or tap prompt) to collect resources.
  • Survive: Hunger & thirst drop over time. Stay alive!
